# Cancellation Policy

**PeakGear Fitness - Order Cancellations**

### Cancellation Window
- Orders can be cancelled within **4 hours** of placement
- Orders already processed or shipped cannot be cancelled

### How to Cancel
Contact our support team immediately with your order number.

### Refunds
- Cancelled orders before processing = full refund
- Cancelled after processing but before shipping = refund minus 10% restocking fee
