# Damaged Items & Defective Products Policy

**PeakGear Fitness**

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

### What We Offer
- Free replacement (preferred)
- Full refund
- Partial refund (if you want to keep lightly damaged item)

### Requirements
- Report within **7 days** of delivery
- Provide photos of damage + packaging
- Item must not show signs of use

### Process
1. Contact support with order number + photos
2. We will review within 24 hours
3. Approved replacements ship within 2 business days
