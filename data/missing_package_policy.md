# Missing Package Policy

**PeakGear Fitness - Missing Packages**

### If Your Order Says "Delivered" But You Haven't Received It

1. Wait **2 business days** after the "delivered" scan before reporting
2. Check with neighbors, apartment office, or porch cameras
3. Contact support with your order number

### If Carrier Lost the Package
- We will investigate with the carrier (usually 3-5 business days)
- If confirmed lost, we will **reship the order at no cost** or issue a full refund
- High-value orders ($150+) get priority tracking and insurance
