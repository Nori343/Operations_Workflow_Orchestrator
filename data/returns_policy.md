# Returns Policy

**PeakGear Fitness - Returns**

We want you to love your purchase. If you’re not satisfied, you may return most items within **30 days** of delivery.

### Eligibility
- Items must be in original condition with tags attached
- Used items (e.g. worn apparel, opened supplements) are generally not eligible
- Personalized or custom orders are non-returnable
- Clearance and final sale items are non-returnable

### Return Window
- Standard: 30 days from delivery date
- Premium members: 45 days
- Damaged items: See Damaged Items Policy

### Process
1. Contact support with your order number
2. Receive return shipping label (we cover return shipping for defective items)
3. Ship item back within 14 days of receiving label
4. Refund issued within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

**Note:** Original shipping costs are non-refundable except in cases of our error.
