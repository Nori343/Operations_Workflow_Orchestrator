# Shipping Policy

**PeakGear Fitness Shipping**

We ship from our warehouse in **Carlsbad, California**.

### Shipping Options
- **Standard Shipping**: 3–7 business days ($9.99)
- **Express Shipping**: 2–4 business days ($19.99)
- **Free Shipping**: Orders over $150 (standard shipping)

### International Shipping
We ship to Canada, UK, Australia, and select EU countries. Additional fees and customs apply.

### Order Tracking
All orders receive tracking information once shipped. You can track via the link in your confirmation email.
